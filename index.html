Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept walk-ins?

We recommend booking in advance to guarantee your preferred time, but we'll do our best to accommodate walk-ins based on availability.

Is parking really free?

Yes! We have ample free parking right at our location. No meters, no stress—just park and relax.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, debit, Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.

What's your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling.

Do you provide direct billing for insurance?

We provide official RMT receipts that you can submit to your insurance company. Many clients get reimbursed quickly through their extended health benefits.

Can I book multiple services in one visit?

Absolutely! Many clients combine treatments like a facial and massage. Call us at (905) 201-8005 and we'll create a custom spa package for you.
