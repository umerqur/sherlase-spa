Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Busy Beauty Menu?

The Busy Beauty Menu is Sheri's new collection of express spa experiences, designed for women who want luxury spa results on a busy schedule. Weekly appointment spots are limited, so we recommend booking early.

Can I buy this as a gift?

Yes. A Busy Beauty experience makes a lovely gift. Mention it is a gift in your booking notes and our team will arrange the details with you.

Do I need to book ahead?

Yes, we recommend booking ahead. Submit your request online or call us, and we will confirm your appointment time with you.

Is parking available?

Yes. Free parking is available right at our Markham location.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash, Debit, Visa, Mastercard, and American Express, as well as secure online payment when you book.

What is your cancellation policy?

We respectfully ask for 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment.
